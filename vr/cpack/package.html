The scene content is divided into Content Packs (hence cpack). Each content pack is a self-contained library for rendering a scene from some dataset of a certain type. For instance, the cpack vr.cpack.space can load and display astronomical data.

The cpacks should be general enough to work with different datasets where possible, and scale up and down nicely. For instance, the cpack.space should facilitate loading large or small star databases. The cpack.meteoro could describe Earth's or Mars's weather.
